üéØ The Problem with Traditional Budgeting Apps

Most personal finance apps suffer from the same fundamental issues:

  • Data Lock-in ‚Äî Your financial data lives on someone else’s servers
  • Subscription Fatigue ‚Äî Another monthly bill that never goes away
  • Limited Customization ‚Äî You’re stuck with their interface and features
  • Privacy Concerns ‚Äî Your most sensitive data is stored in the cloud
  • Vendor Lock-in ‚Äî Switching tools means losing years of financial history

Even YNAB, despite being excellent software, falls into these traps. At $14.99/month ($179.88/year), it’s not just expensive ‚Äî it’s a lifetime commitment to a service you don’t control. That’s nearly $1,800 over 10 years!
